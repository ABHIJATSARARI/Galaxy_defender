Galaxy Defender

Instructions

You are a member of an elite space force that is defending the galaxy from a rogue AI that has taken over the space stations. You must fight through the endless space, destroying the AI's drones, satellites, and weapons, while protecting your allies and bases. How many enemies can you defeat?

  • Use the left and right arrow keys or mouse to move your spaceship.
  • Press the spacebar or click the mouse to shoot lasers.
  • Avoid getting hit by enemy fire or obstacles.
  • Destroy enemies and collect items to increase your score.
  • Watch your health bar and don't let it reach zero.
